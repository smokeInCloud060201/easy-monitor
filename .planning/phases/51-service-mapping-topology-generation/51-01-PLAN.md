# Plan 51-01: Service Topology Endpoint

<objective>
Expose `GET /api/apm/topology` inside the Master Service executing a native ClickHouse join deriving directed service edges from span hierarchies organically. 
</objective>

<requirements>
- ClickHouse queries must be bounded temporally (e.g. `now() - INTERVAL 1 HOUR` or customizable via query parameters).
- The JSON payload must strictly model a directional Graph consisting of structural "Edge" nodes `{source, target, call_count, avg_duration_ns, error_count}`.
</requirements>
